My name is Begum, and I am an instructional designer and learning engineer originally from Turkey. I have a background in education, and I love to explore new ways of learning. I believe in education's transformative power, but the landscape of education is changing fast - there needs to be a profound understanding and application of technical skills in online education. Online education can offer vast opportunities for learning from anywhere any time - more so from face-to-face learning. As an aspiring instructional designer and novice programmer, my goal is to build on my education background by learning programming and therefore improve/develop online education tools and materials to serve better for the learners of the future.
